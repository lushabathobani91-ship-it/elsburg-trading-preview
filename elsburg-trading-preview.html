Rooted in Elsburg's Trade History

Elsburg Trading Co (Pty) Ltd is a privately owned entity serving the local neighborhood. Located right on the corner of Voortrekker Street, we are an integral part of Germiston's mixed commercial environment.

Whether you are a resident tackling a home DIY project or a small contractor working within the Ekurhuleni area, our doors are open. We pride ourselves on practical advice, honest trading, and carrying the goods that keep our local economy moving.
